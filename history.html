First Lumière Core Team
2023 - 2024

The Lumière Club was first established year 2017 and
it has grown and developed into one of the major clubs
in the FEU Diliman Senior High School Department.
Lumière was created to help improve and enhance
the knowledge and the skills of students when it
comes to multimedia arts. Here, members engage
in activities such as showcasing their photography,
editing, and other multimedia related interests.

Now, Lumière is recognized as FEU Diliman's Official Media.

Huwag Mo Akong Tularan
2023

In June 2023, The Lumière Club, together with The Diliman Lit Club
participated in the Animoflix Short Film Festival during
De La Salle Laguna's University Vision-Mission Week.

Huwag Mo Akong Tularan won the Best Film award and the People's Choice Award.
